Organize the world's information in a way which makes it more immediately accessible to everyone in a cozy environment. Having breadth of knowledge in one place is a completely different experience from running everywhere online in order to get it.

Presenting data in a way which makes it easier to access all of any single corpus. Unique datasets such as Metropolitan Museum of Art, The Smithsonian, or MIT OCW each have their own uniquely indexed databases so that the user-interfaces for each implementation can be made to fully guide the data to the researcher.

In the case where data is structured under a framework such as Mediawiki, it follows the framework's indexing while building a user-interface which does its best to bring out what the framework offers. In the case of Mediawiki datasets, a Table of Contents is created as a user-interface for accessing all of any single wiki's pages. This is something that naturally cannot happen in any live wikipedia page whether it be wikipedia itself or fandom.com pages, so the library serves to take already established data sets and repurpose them to be more readable.

Use curatorial guidance to provide the most useful information by pruning queries or datasets in a way which still preserves overall completeness. For example, people will not miss records of museum objects with no media or any records where media quality is underserved due to copyright policies.

Skirt the gray area with grace for preservational progress. A lot of the commercial world takes itself to the grave even after its days of publication, although some change is already happening. Commercial preservation that we have never had the opportunity to keep record of until now without overburden of bandwidth consumption is magazines. Retromags is an organization which preserves old magazines, and very few companies have asked them to kindly not handle their publications. Not just traditional media, but also digital media from the commercial world can benefit from preservational efforts since they are usually what inspires people to become creators in the modern world.
